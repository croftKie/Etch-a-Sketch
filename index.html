How to Play

Hover your cursor over the etch-a-sketch to start drawing. You're always drawing, so be careful creating your masterpiece.

Choose a new colour with the colour picker

Choose between draw mode to create new shapes and erase mode to remove them

Use the opacity slider to make your lines darker or lighter

User the size slider to make your etch-a-sketch more detailed, or less.
